## Your Store, In Front of Every Bubble Tea Drinker in Auckland

Bubble Tea News is a free weekly broadsheet written for — and read by — Auckland's bubble tea retailers and their customers. Every issue carries a handful of ad slots. One of them could be yours.

## Why It Works

- **Right audience, no waste.** Picked up at the counter of the stores you're trying to reach — not scrolled past in a feed.
- **Weekly refresh.** New issue, new placement, every Friday. Long-running slots build recognition instead of fading after one run.
- **Print and digital.** Same ad appears in the physical broadsheet stacked at pickup stores and in the online edition at bubbletea.nz.
- **It gets read.** The paper's satire and columns give people a reason to actually pick it up and flip through — your ad rides along with that attention, not against it.

## Ad Formats

**Sponsor Box**
The format you'll have seen in print — store name, one-line pitch, and a stat callout (queue times, flavor match, sweetness data, whatever's true and worth bragging about). Sized to match the paper's card format, so it reads as part of the page, not an interruption.

**Classifieds Line**
A short listing in the classifieds strip — cheapest way in, good for one-off promos or hiring notices.
