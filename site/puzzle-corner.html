The Four Puzzlers

  1. Ben sits immediately left of Ava; Ben drinks water.
  2. The coffee drinker sits immediately left of Cleo.
  3. Cleo drinks juice. Dmitri isn't in seat 1. Ava doesn't drink coffee.

Answer

Ben — seat 1, water. Dmitri — seat 3, coffee. Cleo — seat 4, juice. Ava — seat 2, the fourth drink, never actually named in the original clues, so no invented answer there. The seat split only resolves one way once Dmitri is ruled out of seat 1.

— Ken Endo, Issue 2
