In Memoriam

The Off-Peak Discount Hour

by Skeletor

Once, between two and three each afternoon, this district's tea counters offered a modest discount to whoever wandered in during the lull. That era has ended. I am reliably informed the discount was "no longer sustainable." I am less reliably informed, by absolutely no one, that a certain villain spent several afternoons testing one counter's patience with lengthy, unnecessary questions during that exact hour. The two are, I assure you, unrelated. Grieve the lull if you liked it. I did not. I filled it.
