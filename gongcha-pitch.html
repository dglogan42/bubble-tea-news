Bubble Tea News

Partnership Proposal
Prepared for: Gong Cha New Zealand
July 2026

A weekly in-store read for Auckland's bubble tea customers — proposing Gong Cha as founding retail partner

What Is Bubble Tea News

Bubble Tea News is a free weekly single-sheet publication (double-sided A4), written in the spirit of hospitality-trade freesheets like Coffee News: a short, characterful read — local trivia, short humour columns, a puzzle, small classifieds — sized to be read in the few minutes a customer waits for their order. It's placed at the counter or on tables, complimentary to customers, and funded by small local advertising placements rather than a cover price.

Why Partner With Us

Free customer engagement

Gives waiting customers something to read at no cost to the store — no setup, no staff training, no ongoing effort.

Fresh content, every week

New puzzle, new columns, new jokes each issue — customers have a reason to pick up the next one.

A branded panel that's actually yours

A dedicated promotional panel each issue for Gong Cha news, seasonal drinks, or loyalty offers — not a generic ad slot.

Local, independent, low-risk

No subscription, no exclusivity lock-in to start — a trial run across a small number of Auckland stores is all we're asking for.

A note on content: several columns are satirical character-voice pieces written in the style of real public figures (e.g. Nixon, Jobs, Robin Leach, Lewis Hamilton, Rebecca Black). These are clearly-labelled parody/satire, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or written by the people named, and none represent real quotes, real products, or real endorsements. We're flagging this openly so there are no surprises about what's being distributed in-store — happy to adjust the lineup per issue if Gong Cha would prefer a different content mix.

The Proposal

We're looking for a founding retail partner to trial Bubble Tea News across a small number of Gong Cha Auckland locations. Specifically, we'd propose:

  • Weekly complimentary supply of printed copies to participating stores
  • A dedicated Gong Cha promotional panel in every issue, at no cost during the trial
  • "Proudly served at Gong Cha" masthead credit for the trial period
  • A short review after 4–6 issues to decide on continuing or expanding

Format & Distribution

Double-sided A4, printed weekly, complimentary to customers. Initial distribution: Auckland retailers, starting with a small pilot group of stores before any wider rollout.

We'd welcome fifteen minutes to talk this through.
