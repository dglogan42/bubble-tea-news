New Zealand's Largest Anime & Manga Convention!

Overload is a two-day convention devoted to celebrating Aotearoa New Zealand’s flourishing anime and manga culture!

Our vibrant event showcases an array of artists who exhibit and sell their creations, and brings together thousands of fans and creative enthusiasts for a fun-filled weekend of international guests, cosplay, competitions, performances, art exhibitions, itasha displays and authentic collectible goodies – whether you’re a dedicated fan or just curious, there’s something for everyone to enjoy!

While we plan for our 2026 event, follow our socials @overloadnz for the latest updates and news!
