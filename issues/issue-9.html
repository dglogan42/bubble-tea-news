Bubble Tea Trivia · Did You Know?

Two Pearls, Two Entirely Different Chemistries

Classic tapioca pearls are cooked starch; "popping boba" is a completely different technology — a thin gel skin formed by dripping fruit juice into a calcium bath, the same spherification trick used in cocktail garnishes, repurposed for a straw.

Travel · Vacation Log, Day 14

Like, We Just Wanted To See One (1) Waterfall, Man

Hiked forty minutes to the lookout, and there's a guy in a rain-cape mask standing right where the track splits, going "turn back." Scoob says "Rhaterfall?" and I'm like, buddy, that's the whole itinerary. Turned out to be the campground owner, diverting hikers around a wasps' nest he hadn't gotten around to reporting. Free hot chips for the detour, though. Worth it. Mostly.

Markets · The Pearl Index

Umbrella Correlation, Statistically Real, Practically Useless

Priya Anand, The Pearl Index
